About

Hi, I'm Amy Lee. I have recently returned home to Nashville after traveling for a bit. I lived in Boston for a year, which is where I developed my passion for technology and its great ability to change global problems. I have been known to solo travel throughout Nepal and Puerto Rico to gain perspective and experience a new place. After returning to Nashville I decided to make my dreams a reality and enrolled in the Nashville Software School to master the skills I need to use technology to affect positive change in the world.

A few of my favorite things: pumpkin coffee, storytelling, Rhett Butler, fly-fishing, coding, TED talks, the banjo, LOTR, Dario Marianelli, global education, ping-pong, machine learning, travels, gender equality, physics, books, books, books.
